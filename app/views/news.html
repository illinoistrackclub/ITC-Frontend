ITC Set to Begin Its Track Season
ITC Web Development Team - 1/6/2017 10:00:00

Winter is a difficult time for most Track and Field athletes. Being on the cusp of the season, it can be hard for athletes to stay motivated to train. Luckily, your ITC web development team is here to turn those woes to wishes. The ITC Web Development has been hard at work making a sleek, new, functional website for your reading pleasure. The process is still on-going, so please have patience with the site for the time being. In the meantime, make sure to report any errors to the new webmaster, Tyler Splitt. His contact info can be found on the contacts page.

Stay tuned for more riveting coverage of ITC's season, and more website improvements as well! We look forward to a fun and successful season!
