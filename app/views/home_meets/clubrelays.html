Cost
$10 per individual event and $20 per relay team (team cap at $350).
Payment is due the day of the meet. Please make checks payable to "Illinois Track and Field Club".
Facility
The meet will be held at the University of Illinois Armory (505 East Armory Avenue, Champaign, IL 61820), a 200 meter indoor track. Spikes are permitted. Batons and starting blocks will be provided.
After 5pm and on weekends, with the exception of lots or spaces labeled as enforced 24/7, there are no parking restrictions for University lots or spaces. Private parking is enforced as posted. City of Champaign meters are enforced as posted.
Parking is available near the Armory in lot C9 to the north; *NOT* E12 directly to the south; E18, E19, and E15 3 blocks to the south; and E2 4 blocks to the southeast. Street parking is available on 4th street to the west (City of Champaign meters enforced 7am ‐ 9pm Mon ‐ Sat), 6th street to the east, and Peabody & Pennsylvania to the south.
There is plenty of parking for Armory events within 3‐4 blocks. Team buses shall drop off and pick up athletes on 4th Street or Armory Ave. Buses should park in lot E15 at the corner of 4th St. & Pennsylvania Ave. Team buses should not drop off or pick up on Gregory Ave. on the south side of the Armory building..

Scoring
Relay events are scored 10-8-6-5-4-3-2-1, with only one relay team per school per event scoring. You may enter as many relay teams as you like. Individual events are scored as two person relays, with the top two marks from each team added together to determine the team score (except for mile, in which case the top four marks are added together). You do not have to declare your scorers in advance. Relay teams must be single-sex.
