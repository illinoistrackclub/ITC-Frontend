Illinois Club Relays
A special thanks to the 29 teams and over 700 athletes who made the 2017 Illinois Club Relays yet again the biggest meet ever! Each year this meet gets bigger and more competitive, as proven by the 6 new meet records broken this year. We look forward to seeing all the teams compete next year at the 2018 Illinois Club Relays on February 24th, 2018!

Cost
$10 per individual event and $20 per relay team (team cap at $350).
Payment is due the day of the meet. Please make checks payable to "Illinois Track and Field Club".
Facility
The meet will be held at the University of Illinois Armory (505 East Armory Avenue, Champaign, IL 61820), a 200 meter indoor track. Spikes are permitted. Batons and starting blocks will be provided. Parking will be available at Huff Hall (directly south of the Armory).
Schedule of Events
The women’s race of each event will precede the men’s race. The meet will be run on a rolling schedule. Both running and field events will begin at 10:00 AM.

Scoring
Relay events are scored 10-8-6-5-4-3-2-1, with only one relay team per school per event scoring. You may enter as many relay teams as you like. Individual events are scored as two person relays, with the top two marks from each team added together to determine the team score (except for mile, in which case the top four marks are added together). You do not have to declare your scorers in advance. Relay teams must be single-sex.
