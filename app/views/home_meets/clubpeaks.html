Illinois Club Peaks
Thank you to all those who braved the weather and came out to the inaugural Illinois Club Peaks! We hope to see you all and many more come out next year and try to break all of the newly set meet records at the 2018 edition of Illinois Club Peaks!
2017 Club Peaks Results
Registration Info
Please contact Sean Bruyere, meet coordinator, at seanef2@illinois.edu or at illinoistfclub+meetcoord@gmail.com
Cost
$10 per individual event and $20 per relay team (team cap at $300).
Payment is due the day of the meet. Please make checks payable to "Illinois Track and Field Club".
Facility
The meet will be held at the University of Illinois Outdoor Track & Field Complex (1300 W. St. Mary's Rd, Urbana, Illinois), a 400 meter outdoor track. Spikes are permitted. Bring your own batons! Starting blocks will be provided. Parking is located in the grass area immediately west of Illinois Field, in the Richard T. Ubben Basketball Complex or at the Bielfeldt Athletic Administration Building. In case of inclement weather additional parking is usually available in the State Farm Center East Quadrants. Handicap parking spots are located within this allotment at any of the locations listed.
Schedule of Events
The women’s race of each event will precede the men’s race. The meet will be run on a rolling schedule. Running events will begin at 12:00 PM. Field events will begin at 12:00 PM with Shot Put and Long Jump. Discus will follow Shot Put and Triple Jump will follow Long Jump.

Scoring
Events are scored 10-8-6-5-4-3-2-1, any number of individuals but only one relay per team can score in each event. You may enter as many relay teams and athletes as you like. You do not have to declare your scorers in advance. Relay teams must be single-sex.
